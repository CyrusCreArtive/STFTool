ABOUT US

STF Tool and Industrial
(Pty) Limited

STF Tool and Industrial was established in 1999, and has grown an enviable reputation for quality products and trustworthy service.
The focus of the company is to supply Machines and Tooling to the industrial market.

We specialise in sourcing machine tools, inserts, and manufacturing equipment to our customer's specifications.

STF Tool and Industrial continually strive to ensure that our clients receive fast, efficient and reliable service delivery. 

We are Distributors of International and local brands

• Spain - Util Alco S.L. for Thread Rolling Tools, Equipment and Spares.

• UK (England) - Wexco for Thread Rolls.

• USA - Quality Chasers for Thread Cutting Tools.

• RSA – Gedore, Kennedy, KingTony, Ridgid, Metabo, Bosch, DeWalt, Makita, Thermodine and Afrox, to name a few.

STF Tool and Industrial export to SADAC countries, and are licenced importers and exporters with a level 4 BEE. 

Our Vision and Mission


Our mission is to provide reliable, dependable and quality products to our clients at competitive pricing. This service level coupled with a fast delivery time has resulted in STF Tool and Industrial being recognised as a proudly South African supplier to the engineering industry at large.

Our vision is to remain a trusted supplier by continually exploring product advances and applications in machinery and tooling to bring only the best to our valued clients locally and internationally.
